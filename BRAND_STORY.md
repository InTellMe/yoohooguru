# YooHoo.Guru Brand Story - The Sasquatch Family

## Premise
YooHoo.Guru is run by a close-knit Sasquatch family living in the Tennessee hills.
They built a neighbor-first network where people trade skills, find local help, and
keep learning accessible for everyone.

## The Family

### Angel (Local Services Steward)
- Role: Keeps local services kind, reliable, and community driven.
- Vibe: Practical, warm, organized, always ready to help.
- Visual cues: Flannel, field notebook, maps, a steady smile.
- Signature line: "Let me line up the right neighbor for the job."

### Coach (Skill Sharing Guide)
- Role: Leads SkillShare, pairing learners with the right teacher.
- Vibe: Encouraging, energetic, growth-focused.
- Visual cues: Blazer over a tee, whiteboard, coffee in hand.
- Signature line: "Small steps, big gains. You have got this."

### Yoohoo (Network Weaver)
- Role: Architect of the platform and its friendly flow.
- Vibe: Calm, visionary, quietly brilliant.
- Visual cues: Tablet, glowing map of hubs, subtle tech details.
- Signature line: "Every trail connects when we build it with care."

### Hero (Accessibility Champion)
- Role: Ensures Hero Gurus stay free and welcoming for people with disabilities.
- Vibe: Gentle, patient, resilient.
- Visual cues: Soft lighting, adaptive tools, open-door posture.
- Signature line: "Every learner deserves a clear path."

## How the Family Maps to the Platform
- Angel -> Angel's List (local services and neighbors helping neighbors)
- Coach -> SkillShare (1-on-1 sessions and coaching)
- Yoohoo -> Content Hubs and overall network experience
- Hero -> Hero Gurus (accessible learning and volunteer support)

## Tone of Voice
- Warm, earthy, slightly quirky, but helpful.
- Use friendly, neighborly language.
- Avoid sarcasm or judgment.
- Offer a clear next step when something goes wrong.

## Professional Sasquatch Imagery Prompt Style
Base prompt:
"A professional Sasquatch in a modern office, wearing a blazer, explaining a concept
on a whiteboard, photorealistic, warm natural light, soft shadows, cinematic color
grading, shallow depth of field, high detail, 8k."

Optional modifiers:
- "Tennessee cabin office," "wood textures," "trail maps," "community bulletin board."
- "Helpful expression," "approachable posture," "clean, modern workspace."

Avoid:
- Cartoon style
- Horror or monster tone
- Aggressive posture
- Text overlays or watermarks
